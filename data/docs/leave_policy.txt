Leave Policy Summary

1. Leave types
- Earned leave
- Sick leave
- Casual leave
- Bereavement leave
- Unpaid leave

2. Leave approval
- Short leave: manager approval required.
- Leave longer than 2 working days: manager and HR approval required.
- Sick leave above 3 days may require a medical certificate.

3. Carry forward
- Unused earned leave may be carried forward up to the annual cap defined by HR each year.
- Leave encashment is governed by the employment contract and local labor rules.

4. Request workflow
- Apply in the leave portal at least 3 working days in advance for planned leave.
- Mark urgent or emergency leave as soon as possible.
- Approved leave is visible in the team calendar.

5. Notes
- Public holidays do not count as leave.
- Overlapping leave requests are resolved by the reporting manager.
