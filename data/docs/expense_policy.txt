Expense Reimbursement Policy

1. Eligible expenses
- Local travel for work
- Client meals within policy limits
- Office supplies purchased with approval
- Internet reimbursement for remote work, if eligible

2. Submission rules
- Submit claims within 30 days of the expense date.
- Attach receipts for any expense above the receipt threshold.
- Add the business justification and project code.

3. Approval path
- The manager reviews first.
- Finance performs policy validation.
- High-value claims may need additional approval.

4. Reimbursement timeline
- Approved claims are usually reimbursed in the next payment cycle.
- Incorrect or missing documents can delay settlement.

5. Common rejection reasons
- Missing receipt
- Duplicate claim
- Incorrect cost center
- Unsupported personal expense
