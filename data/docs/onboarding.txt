Enterprise Onboarding Guide

1. First-day checklist
- Complete identity verification and HR forms.
- Collect laptop, access card, and workstation details from IT.
- Join the welcome call with the hiring manager and buddy.
- Review the code of conduct and security policy.

2. Required documents
- Government ID
- Address proof
- Bank details for payroll
- Signed offer letter

3. Access request process
Employees should raise access requests through the service desk. Typical approvals are needed for:
- email account
- internal knowledge base
- finance portal
- project repositories

4. Probation review
- Probation lasts 90 days unless specified otherwise.
- Managers schedule a review at the end of the probation period.
- Feedback should be documented in the HR portal.

5. Useful tip
New joiners should complete mandatory training within the first two weeks.
